Common Questions

Things People Usually
Ask Before Reaching Out.

How much does a website cost?

Our websites start at $1,000 for a clean, multi-page custom site and go up based on complexity, number of pages, and any e-commerce or automation requirements. Everything is a one-time fee — no subscriptions, ever.

How long does a project take?

Most websites are completed in two to four weeks from the time we receive your content and sign-off on the design. Larger projects with e-commerce or automation integrations may take four to six weeks.

Do I need to provide my own content?

Ideally yes — your photos, your story, your menu or tour list. If you don't have content ready, we can help you structure it and write copy that works. Just let us know upfront.

What if I need changes after launch?

Every project comes with a 30-day post-launch window for minor tweaks and fixes at no additional cost. After that, we offer ongoing support through our Maintenance & Performance retainer or as individual requests.

Do you work with businesses outside the Bahamas?

Absolutely. We're Caribbean-focused but work with businesses across the entire region — Jamaica, Barbados, Trinidad, Cayman Islands, and anywhere in between. We do everything remotely.

What's AIO and do I need it?

AIO (AI Optimization) ensures your business appears when people ask AI tools like ChatGPT or Gemini for recommendations. If your customers are starting to use AI search — and they are — you need it. We bake it into every build.

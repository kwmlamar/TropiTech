Tourism / Hospitality

Bimini Island Tours

A full multi-page website for a Bahamian local guide bringing the magic of Bimini to the world. The challenge: capture the raw beauty and authentic spirit of island life while delivering a digital experience sharp enough to compete with large booking platforms.

We built the entire site hand-coded with GSAP-powered animations, a multi-step booking form, a curated tour listings page, and a full image gallery — all optimized for mobile and fast enough to load on the island's real-world connectivity.

Food & Beverage

Chicken Souse For Life

Not just a restaurant website — a love letter to Bahamian food culture. Chicken Souse For Life needed a site that matched its heart: warm, rich, deeply rooted in local tradition, and proud of what it serves. We delivered an editorial-style experience that tells the story before the customer ever reads the menu.

The design features a warm, cinematic palette with dramatic food photography framing, immersive section layouts, and editorial copy that makes you feel the steam rising from the pot. The result is a digital presence as memorable as the dish itself.
